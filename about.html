About Our Farm

Located in Tshiozwi Village, Makhado, Mukangane Farm is committed to sustainable farming and community empowerment.

We supply both commercial markets and local communities with high-quality produce, poultry, and livestock. Our core values: honesty, hard work, and respect for the land.
